Post-Op Care

Care instructions that make recovery easier to follow

Good aftercare supports comfort, healing, and long-term success. These instructions are intentionally practical and easy to follow.

The first 24 to 48 hours

  • - Rest and follow the medication guidance provided.
  • - Keep the surgical area clean and avoid disturbing it.
  • - Use cold compresses when recommended.

During healing

  • - Eat the foods you were advised to eat.
  • - Keep follow-up visits so healing can be monitored.
  • - Call the clinic if anything feels unusual or unclear.
