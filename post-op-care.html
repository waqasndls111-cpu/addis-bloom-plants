Aftercare

Post-operative care for a smooth recovery

Patients do better when the aftercare plan is simple and clear. This page can be expanded into a downloadable instruction sheet, video guide, or patient handout section.

Immediately after treatment

  • Follow the clinic’s written instructions exactly.
  • Keep the area clean as directed.
  • Avoid disturbing the surgical site.
  • Contact the clinic if you are unsure about symptoms.

The first week

  • Plan softer meals if recommended.
  • Attend follow-up checks on time.
  • Use your prescribed medication carefully.
  • Report unusual swelling, pain, or bleeding promptly.
