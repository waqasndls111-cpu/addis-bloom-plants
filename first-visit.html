First Visit

What to expect on your first visit

This page helps new patients prepare for the consultation, understand the flow, and arrive with the right documents and expectations.

Your appointment may include

  1. 1. A conversation about your goals and concerns.
  2. 2. Clinical examination and imaging review.
  3. 3. A treatment recommendation or next-step plan.
  4. 4. Discussion of timing, cost, and aftercare.

Bring with you

  • Any recent dental records or x-rays.
  • Details of insurance or financing documents.
  • Medication list and medical history notes.
  • A list of questions you want answered.
