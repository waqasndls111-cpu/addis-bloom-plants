First Visit

What happens at your first consultation

Your first visit is about understanding your concerns, reviewing your dental history, and mapping out the most sensible next step. We keep the process calm, respectful, and efficient.

1. Welcome and history

We gather details about your symptoms, past treatment, and goals.

2. Exam and imaging

A clinical review helps us understand the anatomy and treatment options.

3. Plan and next steps

We explain what is recommended, what can wait, and how the timeline works.
