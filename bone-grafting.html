Foundation

Bone grafting that prepares the jaw for a more predictable implant outcome

When the jaw needs reinforcement, bone grafting can help create the foundation required for successful implant placement. The goal is always to improve long-term stability and treatment predictability.

Why grafting may be recommended

  • - To rebuild volume where bone has resorbed after tooth loss.
  • - To improve support for implant placement.
  • - To make future restorative results more stable and predictable.
  • - To support treatment planning for Addis Ababa patients with complex cases.
