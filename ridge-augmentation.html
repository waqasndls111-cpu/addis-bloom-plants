Ridge augmentation for rebuilding lost jaw contour before restorative treatment

Ridge augmentation is used when the jaw has lost height or width after tooth loss, extraction, or long-term wear. Rebuilding the ridge can improve the foundation for future implants or restorations.

This treatment can help with

  • Re-establishing better jaw shape.
  • Creating more predictable implant support.
  • Improving the look of the gum and bone contour.
  • Supporting staged treatment planning.
