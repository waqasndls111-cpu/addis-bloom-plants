FAQ

Answers patients usually want before starting treatment

These questions are written to reduce uncertainty and help you understand the treatment flow, healing expectations, and the role of supporting procedures.

How do I know if I am a candidate for implants?

A consultation, clinical exam, and imaging review help determine whether implants are appropriate or whether grafting, a sinus lift, or a different restorative plan is needed first.

Is implant treatment painful?

Most patients are surprised by how manageable the process feels when it is carefully planned and clearly explained. Comfort guidance is part of every visit.

How long does treatment take?

Timing depends on the number of implants, the presence of grafting, and the healing response. During consultation we outline a realistic sequence for your specific case.

Do you treat patients from outside Addis Ababa?

Yes. Many patients travel for specialist implant care and appreciate that the clinic is organized around clear planning and efficient follow-up.
