Upper Jaw Support

Sinus lift procedures for upper-jaw implant planning

A sinus lift may be considered when there is not enough bone height in the upper back jaw for implant placement. The aim is to build a more reliable foundation before the implant stage.

Common reasons it is discussed

  • Upper molar or premolar implant support is limited.
  • Bone height needs improvement before planning.
  • A staged restoration approach is recommended.
  • Long-term implant predictability is a priority.
