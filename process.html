Treatment Journey

A full treatment journey from consultation to final restoration

The process is intentionally structured so patients know what to expect before they commit. Each stage reduces uncertainty and helps the final result feel more predictable.

1. Consultation

Discuss your goals, review your dental history, and identify whether implants or a supporting procedure is the right next step.

2. Imaging and planning

A more detailed review helps determine bone quality, tooth positions, and restorative options.

3. Surgical phase

Implant placement or bone preparation is completed with attention to comfort and surgical planning.

4. Healing and checkups

The jaw and soft tissue are monitored while integration and recovery take place.

5. Final restoration

The final crown, bridge, or full arch restoration is fitted and reviewed for comfort and appearance.

What makes the process feel easier

  • One clear conversation before treatment starts.
  • Shared expectations about timing and healing.
  • Practical instructions for before and after surgery.
  • A direct path to booking if you are ready to move forward.
