Orthognathic Jaw Surgery

The purpose of orthognathic surgery is to reposition a misaligned jaw to ensure that teeth meet properly and the jaw functions effectively.

What is Orthognathic Surgery?

Orthognathic surgery, also known as corrective jaw surgery, is a type of surgical procedure performed by oral and maxillofacial surgeons in conjunction with orthodontists to correct skeletal and dental irregularities in the jaw and face.
