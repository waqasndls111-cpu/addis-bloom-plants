What is a Tooth Extraction?

If a tooth has been severely damaged due to injury, accident, or decay, it may need to be extracted. Tooth extraction is often referred to as pulling a tooth and is the procedure where a tooth is removed entirely to ensure a healthy smile.

A common form of tooth extraction in adults is the removal of wisdom teeth.
