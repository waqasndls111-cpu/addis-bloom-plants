Impacted Canines

Teeth are called impacted if they fail to erupt and pass through the gums. Teeth that have only partially erupted through the gums are also considered to be impacted teeth.

What Causes A Tooth To Be Impacted?

There are several reasons that a tooth can become impacted and fail to fully erupt. The most common causes of tooth impaction are insufficient space in the jaw and overcrowding, misalignment, or conflicting position of teeth in the jaw.
