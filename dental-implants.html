Dental implants in Addis Ababa for lasting stability and natural function

Dental implants replace missing teeth with a root-like foundation placed in the jaw. They are often recommended for patients who want a stronger, more natural-feeling solution than removable options.

Book a Consultation Read FAQs

Why patients choose implants

  • They can improve chewing comfort and bite stability.
  • They support a natural-looking restoration design.
  • They help preserve the shape of the jaw over time.
  • They are designed as a fixed solution, not a removable one.

What the treatment typically involves

Assessment

Clinical exam, imaging review, and discussion of your restorative goals.

Placement

The implant is placed in a planned position that supports healing and function.

Healing

The bone integrates with the implant over time under monitored follow-up.

Restoration

A crown, bridge, or full-arch restoration completes the smile.
