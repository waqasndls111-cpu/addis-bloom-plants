Insurance & Financing

Clear payment guidance for implant and oral surgery treatment

Every case is different, so we encourage patients to ask about their coverage, staged treatment needs, and payment expectations during the consultation.

What to ask us about

  • - Treatment staging and payment timing.
  • - Which parts of care are part of the plan.
  • - Whether follow-up visits are included.
  • - Any documentation you need for reimbursement discussions.

Practical options

Patients can discuss staged treatment, bank transfer, cash, or clinic-approved payment arrangements. The aim is to keep the conversation clear and respectful.
